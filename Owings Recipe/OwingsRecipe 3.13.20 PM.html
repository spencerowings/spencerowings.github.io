Carrot Ginger Soup

This soup is SOUPer good

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Ginger
  • Cauliflower
  • Chicken broth
  • Coconut Milk
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Spices - Salt, Tumeric, Paprika, Cayenne

Instructions

  1. Sautee onions and garlic with olive oil
  2. Cut carrots and ginger and add with more oil
  3. Cut cauliflower into small bits and add to sautee with all of the spices
  4. Once vegatables are fully cooked, add Chicken Broth and coconut milk and let cook for another 15-20minutes
  5. Use emersion blender to make soup into a puree
  6. Serve and enjoy!
